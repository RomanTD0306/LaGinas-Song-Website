About LaGina

LaGina Sharp Davis is a country-Christian songwriter rooted in the heart of Long Beach, Mississippi. Her writing carries the warmth of front-porch storytelling and the depth of a life grounded in faith.

An English teacher for over twenty years, LaGina has spent her career living inside language — building young minds and choosing words with care. That same intentionality shows up in the lyrics she writes. Her songs blend country and her faith into something personal and lasting.

Outside of the classroom, LaGina is a wife, mother, nature-lover, and 3rd-grade Sunday school teacher. She has over 34 original songs and is actively seeking collaborations with artists and publishers.
