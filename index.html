About LaGina

LaGina Sharp Davis is a country-Christian writer rooted in the heart of Louisiana and Mississippi. Her writing carries the warmth of front-porch storytelling and celebrates the joy of family and friendships.

An English teacher for over twenty years, LaGina has spent her career living inside language — building young minds and choosing words with care. That same intentionality shows up in the lyrics she writes. Her lyrics blend memories, faith, and authenticity into something personal and lasting.

LaGina is a wife, mother, nature-lover, and yoga enthusiast. Along with original lyrics, LaGina is also working on a children's book. She is actively seeking collaborations with artists and publishers.
